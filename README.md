# Reproduced Papers

This repository contains my personal reproductions of research papers, primarily in quantum control and quantum information.

The goal is to better understand published methods by re-implementing simulations, figures, and workflows in a clean and reproducible way.

## Scope

- Quantum control papers
- Quantum information papers
- Reproduction-focused notebooks, scripts, and datasets

## Important Note

The original papers are linked in the corresponding project materials and remain the work of their respective authors.

I am not affiliated with the original authors, journals, institutions, or publishers.

## Current Project

- `SpeedingUpQAwithDephasing/`: reproduction materials for *Speeding Up Quantum Annealing with Engineered Dephasing*
